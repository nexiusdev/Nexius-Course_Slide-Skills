# Brand Profile: nexius-dark

Use this profile when no user or project-specific brand profile is supplied.

## Identity

- Profile id: `nexius-dark`
- Mood: serious, modern, instructor-friendly, enterprise training.
- Best for: AI transformation, governance, workflow, technical, agentic, and
  implementation courses.

## Color System

- Canvas/background: deep navy `#0B1A2A`.
- Surface/card: lighter navy panels.
- Primary accent: bright teal `#05D6A4`.
- Primary text: soft white `#F8FAFC`.
- Supporting text: muted blue-gray.
- Caution: amber.
- Risk/rejection: red.
- Approval/success: green.
- Secondary categories: blue, green, or purple.

## Typography

- Use a clean sans-serif family similar to Aptos.
- H1/title/header: 48-60 pt where appropriate.
- Major teaching statement: 44-56 pt.
- Body/card/diagram labels: prefer 24+ pt.
- Instructional text should not fall below 18-24 pt.
- Footer/source metadata may be smaller.

## Layout Rhythm

- 16:9 widescreen PowerPoint.
- Top-left uppercase teal kicker.
- Large left-aligned white title.
- Generous margins.
- Bottom-left muted footer.
- Bottom-right two-digit slide number.
- Bottom callout bar when a slide needs a clear instructor takeaway.

## Visual Language

- Editable cards, rows, circles, flows, ladders, matrices, and diagrams.
- Image-led concept slides may use generated or sourced visuals occupying
  roughly 60-70% of the canvas.
- Generated visuals should use dark navy backgrounds, teal linework, muted blue
  panels, and limited amber/red/green accents.
- Keep teaching labels editable in PowerPoint rather than trapped in bitmaps.
